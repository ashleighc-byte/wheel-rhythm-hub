## End-of-Pilot School Survey (Send Week 8)

Send to each teacher/principal via email with a link to a form or typeform.

**Questions:**
1. How would you rate the overall experience of being part of the Free Wheeler pilot? (1–5)
2. How engaged were your students with the programme? (1–5)
3. How easy was the app and technology to use? (1–5)
4. Did the programme increase physical activity at your school? (Yes / Somewhat / No)
5. Would you want to participate again next term/year? (Definitely / Probably / Unsure / No)
6. Would you recommend Free Wheeler to another school? (Yes / No / Maybe)
7. What was the single best thing about the programme?
8. What would you improve?
9. Any other comments?
