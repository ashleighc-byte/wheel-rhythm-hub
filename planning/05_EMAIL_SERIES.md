## Track 1: School Recruitment

---

### SR-01 — Cold Outreach to Principal

**To:** Principal
**Subject:** Free bikes. Zero admin. Your students riding — would you be interested?
**Timing:** 8 weeks before season

```
Hi [Principal Name],

My name is Grant and I work with Sport Waikato. I'm reaching out because I think your school would be a great fit for something we're running this term.

Free Wheeler Bike League installs smart Wattbikes in your school — at no cost to you. Students ride whenever they want, earn points, and compete on a leaderboard against other schools. No PE period needed. No teacher time required. No student fees.

We're running our pilot in Term 2 with 7 Waikato schools and we have one spot remaining.

Here's what it involves for your school:
✓ We deliver and install 2 Wattbikes
✓ You sign a simple agreement (takes 5 minutes)
✓ One announcement to students and a QR code poster goes up
✓ From there — it runs itself

Our goal is to get more of your students active — especially those who don't usually play school sport. We'd love [School Name] to be part of it.

Can I give you a quick call this week to tell you more?

Ride on,
Grant Ashl
Programme Lead | Free Wheeler Bike League
Sport Waikato
P: [phone]
E: grant@sportwaikato.org.nz
```

## Track 2: Teacher Onboarding

---

### TO-01 — Welcome to the Programme

**To:** School PE Contact
**Subject:** Welcome to Free Wheeler — everything you need to know
**Timing:** 1 week before season start

```
Hi [Teacher Name],

Welcome to Free Wheeler Bike League! We're so excited to have [School Name] on board for Term 2.

Here's a quick checklist to get ready:

BEFORE WEEK 1:
✓ Wattbikes have been installed — let me know if anything looks off
✓ Print the attached registration QR poster and put it near the bikes
✓ Make one announcement to students: "The Free Wheeler bikes are live. Scan the QR code to join."

THAT'S IT. Seriously — that's all you need to do.

Students handle their own registration, ride when they want, and log their own sessions. You'll be copied into a weekly update email so you can see how your school is tracking.

I'll be in touch weekly with a quick progress check. I'm always available on [phone] or [email] if anything comes up.

Let's go!
Grant
```
